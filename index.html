UVic Web Development and Web Design Club

UVic Web Dev Club is a community that aims to help students bring ideas to life. No matter what facility you're in, or skill set you have, we're here to help.

About

If you're wanting to spend your future in tech then this is the right place to get your foot in the door. Every week we nerd out about interface design, modern web languages, and cool stuff that's happening in the now. We bring in smart people from cool companies, host hackathons, and overall just have a great time.
